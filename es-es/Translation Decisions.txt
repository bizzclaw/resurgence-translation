Several names
Things such as Vault City remain unchanged as to keep consistence with the official fallout games translations
Some names such as crimson caravan or cascade caravan have only had the "caravan" part translated
Names like Salt Wells remain unchanged 

Faction Names
For the sake of consistency in the text messages such as "Wanted by the (faction)", the noun "the" will be removed and instead faction names will have their respective noun in the title
In Spanish, "The" is not a one size fits all solution to naming, so each name will start by either "Los" "La" "El"
Some examples are "El Enclave" "Los Rangers del Desierto" "La República de Nueva California"
Some will not have a noun at all, but these are mainly things like vault city, as no noun fits them nicely

Old world

Courtyard (Vault City)
The vault city courtyard has been changed "vault city plaza", as courtyard when translated is commonly associated with backyards or frontyards in spanish
